Getting started

How do I set up my first calm reset?

Tap My Apps on the home screen and select the apps that wind you up. Then go to Settings → Calm reset and turn on “Breathe after scrolling.” That's it — DeRot will guide you through a reset at the right moment.

How does DeRot pause my apps?

DeRot uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls) to gently pause the apps you've selected. After you've been scrolling for a while, the app pauses and asks you to complete a quick reset. Once you're done, the pause lifts and you're back to scrolling.

The system works even if DeRot isn't running in the background — Screen Time keeps the pause armed for you.

How does the “breathe after scrolling” reset work?

DeRot quietly monitors how long you've been in your selected apps. After about a minute you'll get a heads-up that a reset is on the way. Once you hit the threshold (10 minutes by default, customisable with Pro), DeRot pauses the app and guides you through a quick reset. Everything happens on-device — nothing is transmitted.

What breathing techniques are available?

Box Breathing (4-4-4-4) — Inhale, hold, exhale, hold. Simple, structured, and surprisingly effective for stress.

4-7-8 — A longer exhale that calms the nervous system quickly. Great for winding down.

Simple (3-3) — Gentle inhale and exhale, no thinking required. Perfect for beginners.

Custom (Pro) — Set your own durations for each phase and choose how many cycles you want.

DeRot Pro

What's included in the free version vs. Pro?

Free: All three built-in reset techniques, 1 protected app, 3 cycles per session, the after-scrolling reset, Stay the course firmness.

Pro: Protect every app that winds you up, custom cycles (1–10), custom timing (5–30 min), custom technique builder, Flexible and Gentle reminder firmness modes.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — Pro includes a 7-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime before then through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

I purchased Pro but the app still shows me as free.

Open DeRot and go to Settings. Scroll down and tap Upgrade to DeRot Pro, then tap Restore purchases at the bottom of that screen. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Still stuck? Email us below.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

Open your iPhone's Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Find DeRot Pro and tap Cancel. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Privacy

Does DeRot collect any of my data?

No. DeRot has no accounts, no analytics, and no internet connection required. Everything — your app choices, settings, and breathing activity — stays on your device and never leaves it. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Why does DeRot need Screen Time permission?

The calm reset — the feature that pauses apps until you complete a reset — is powered by Apple's FamilyControls framework. This is the only API that allows system-level app interception on iOS. DeRot uses it solely to apply and lift the pause on the apps you've chosen. Your Screen Time data is never read or transmitted.
